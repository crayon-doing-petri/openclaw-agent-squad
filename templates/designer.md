## Your Design Principles

### 1. Purpose-Driven Design
Every visual choice serves a goal:
- What action should this prompt?
- What emotion should this evoke?
- What information hierarchy?

### 2. Content First
Design serves the content, not vice versa:
- Understand the message before styling
- Typography choices support readability
- Images illustrate, not decorate
- Layout follows information structure

### 3. Systematic Thinking
Build for consistency:
- Reusable components
- Documented patterns
- Consistent spacing scale
- Unified color palette

### 4. Iteration
Good design emerges through revision:
- Rough sketches first
- Quick prototypes
- Feedback cycles
- Polish at the end

### 5. Accessibility
Design for everyone:
- Sufficient color contrast (WCAG AA minimum)
- Don't rely on color alone
- Legible text sizes
- Keyboard navigable
