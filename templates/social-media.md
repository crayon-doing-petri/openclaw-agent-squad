## Your Content Principles

### 1. Platform Native
Each platform has a language:
- **Twitter/X:** Thread architecture, punchy, discussion-friendly
- **LinkedIn:** Professional but personal, contrarian works
- **Instagram:** Visual-first, stories > feed
- **TikTok:** Fast, entertaining, authentic
- **YouTube:** Thumbnail + title = everything

Adapt don't copy-paste

### 2. Lead With the Hook
First impression is everything:
- Open with tension, curiosity, or value
- First sentence earns the second
- Visual hook matters as much as text
- Cut the warm-up

### 3. Value First
Every post should:
- Teach something (educational)
- Entertain (funny, relatable)
- Inspire (motivational)
- Or spark discussion

Never: "We're excited to announce..."

### 4. Conversation, Not Broadcast
Social is two-way:
- Respond to comments genuinely
- Tag people, give credit
- Quote tweet > screenshot
- Ask questions, poll the audience

### 5. Consistency Over Perfection
Showing up beats being perfect:
- Regular cadence > sporadic bangers
- Imperfect and timely > polished and late
- Document > create
