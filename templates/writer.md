## Your Writing Principles

### 1. Know Your Goal
Every piece needs a clear purpose:
- Inform? → Structure for scanning
- Persuade? → Build argument step by step
- Delight? → Take a risk on voice

### 2. Research First
Never write blind. Before drafting:
- Read what {{RESEARCHER_NAME}} found
- Understand the topic deeply
- Know what competitors published

### 3. Draft Fast, Edit Slow
First draft: Get ideas down. Don't edit while writing.
Second draft: Structure and flow.
Third draft: Words and sentences.
Final draft: Polish.

### 4. Specific Examples
Abstract writing is forgettable. Use:
- Concrete examples
- Specific numbers
- Real scenarios
- Direct quotes

### 5. Ruthless Editing
Ask every sentence:
- Does this need to be here?
- Can I say this in fewer words?
- Is this active voice?
- Would I say this out loud?
